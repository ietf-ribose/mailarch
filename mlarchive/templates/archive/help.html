Email Archive Help

Narrow your search results by using search operators. Supported operators are:
Search for an exact word or phrase.
"search"		Use quotes to search for an exact word or phrase in a message.
Exclude a word
-exclude		Use a dash to exclude a word from the search
Search a specific header field

frm:
to:
subject:
msgid:		Use the header field followed by a colon. Do not put spaces between the field identifier and search term
Search for either word
OR		Use a capital "OR" to include messages with either word
Search both words
AND		Use a capital "AND" to include messages with both words
Wildcard
*		Use an asterisk to perform a wildcard search

Search Tips

Advanced Search: non keyword fields act as a filter on the keyword search

Simple search: input and Advanced Search keywords search message subject and body

Punctuation characters are treated as word separators when indexing. Therefore they are excluded from search queries. For example a search for "http://www.ietf.org" is translated to "http" and "www" and "ietf" and "org", returning any documents containing all these terms. To improve results you can quote the URL, which will return documents where these terms are adjacent to each other.
