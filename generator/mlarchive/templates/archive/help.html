Search Query Tips

Punctuation Characters

Punctuation characters are treated as word separators when indexing. Therefore they are excluded from search queries. For example a search for "http://www.ietf.org" is translated to "http" and "www" and "ietf" and "org", returning any documents containing all these terms. To improve results you can quote the URL, which will return documents where these terms are adjacent to each other.
